Instructions to install SUGGESTO for iOS devices


  • 1. Open this link in Safari browser on your iOS device.
  • 2. Click the share button below in the bottom nav bar in browser.
  • 3. Scroll down and find 'Add to HomeScreen' option and click that.
  • 4. Click 'Add' at the top.
  • 5. Hurray!! Suggesto app is now available on your iOS device. You can use that app same as native app.
  • NOTE : We are working on 'Native iOS app on AppStore'. It will be available soon. Until then please bear with us. We offer all the features that are available on app in here too.
