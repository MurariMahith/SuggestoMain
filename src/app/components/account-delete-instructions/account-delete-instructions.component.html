Account Delete Instructions


  • If you want to delete your account. You need to be logged In
  • Navigate to profile page from footer or side nav bar
  • In profile page, select settings section
  • Scroll down and you find 'Delete My Account'. click that.
  • You will receive a confirmation popup asking are you sure you want to delete the account.
  • Click Ok.
  • Please enter the verification string just to make sure you are not accidentally deleting the account. click ok.
  • An alert will be shown telling you your account deletion process is initiated.
  • Click OK and please wait patiently. Your account will be logged out of system and your account will be deleted.

What you will loose when you delete your account


  • You wishlists, ratings and watched lists will be gone
  • All the custom lists you created will be gone
  • All your preferences will be gone
  • Your followers, following, follow requests you sent and follow requests you received will be gone
  • You will be completely new to Suggesto when you try to login again to suggesto.
  • Please read instructions above to delete your account and only delete your account when you really want it.
