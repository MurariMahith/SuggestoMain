Know More About Suggesto!

We all faced a situation where we keep on searching a best movie to watch all day but end up wasting your time, so here we come up with an idea where we suggest a movie on each weekend day and show personalised movies based on your preferences. all the preferences are based on the rating users like you give to those movies. For better inter communication between south indiam movie industries, we suggest movies in English, Telugu, Tamil, Malayam, Kannada(coming soon).

So you just have to decide what genre and language you want to see movies and we got you covered with hand picked movies just for you. We don't take data from IMDB or other services so movie rating is purely based on rating given by users just like you.

Please go through all the features offere by us below for better use of 'Suggesto'

Searching and Filtering
  • Here you can search for your movie based on many search criteria we offer as mentioned below .
  • You can search with
    • Movie title
    • Released Year
    • Cast
    • tags such as 'smash' for hulk,avengers etc.,.
    • Genre
    • Available OTT platform ex: prime,netflix
    • small description or characters in the movie
  • You can also see movie based on filters on "Years", "Genre", "Language".
  • To use all these filters toggle the button on right side of search bar in 'Search page'.
Custom Movie Lists
  • You can also create a custom list of movies and these movie lists can be viewed in movie lists section based on rating.
  • You can delete these lists, you and other users can also rate these list of movies so we will show your list on top if it has good rating.
  • You can see lists created by in profile section.
  • You can select a list to show as series so that we will show them in a serial manner in ascending order based on release year. Ex: joh wick series, Mission impossible series.
  • But you need to log in to use this feature, click here to login.
Movie Lists
